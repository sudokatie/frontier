# Changelog

## [0.1.0] - 2026-02-02

### Added

- Galaxy system with 5 star systems (Lave, Diso, Leesti, Riedquat, Zaonce)
- Trade routes between connected systems
- Market system with prices varying by economy type
- Ship with fuel management and cargo capacity
- Player state with credits and cargo tracking
- Trading mechanics (buy/sell commodities)
- Jump travel with fuel consumption
- Canvas-rendered galaxy map
- Station view with market panel
- Cargo panel showing profit potential
- Status bar with credits, fuel, cargo
- 130 unit tests covering all game logic

### Economy Types

- Agricultural: cheap food
- Industrial: balanced prices
- Mining: cheap minerals
- High-tech: cheap computers

### Starting Conditions

- 100 credits
- 10 LY fuel tank
- 10 ton cargo capacity
- Located at Lave Station
